## Strategy domains

### Product tier strategy
Professional → Desk → Institutional → Enterprise Infrastructure — expanding workflow depth per tier.

### Scaling architecture
Phased readiness for active traders, pro traders, crypto teams, funds, institutions, market makers, treasury, enterprise orgs.

### Infrastructure scaling
Global latency, distributed ingest, regional redundancy, failover, stream partitioning, event processing, uptime guarantees.

### Operational excellence
Support, incident management, uptime monitoring, runbooks, deployment, observability, recovery.

### Go-to-market
Professional traders, crypto desks, research orgs, market makers, treasury, institutional firms — **not** mass retail hype.

### Institutional trust
Uptime, reliability, execution quality, information accuracy, operational consistency, workflow superiority.

### Adoption strategy
Workflow embedding, org dependency, collaboration, operational continuity, intelligence advantages, execution integration.

### Competitive positioning
Bloomberg for crypto · institutional operating infrastructure · operational intelligence layer · execution-aware infrastructure.

### Strategic moats
Proprietary intel, workflow dependency, integrations, operational trust, org embedding, market memory, network effects, infra scale.

### Global operational readiness
Enterprise onboarding, institutional support, global scaling, high-volume events, crisis handling, regulatory adaptation.
